CAMPAIGN

What I’ve Accomplished
No one individual is able to accomplish what many could do together, I’d like to mention what I’ve done as part of the student council couldn't have been done without everyone on the council who worked tirelessly to make incredible events possible.
As part of the student council, we managed to make one of our major school dances affordable to many more students, so much so that we sold out! Events like these begin to demonstrate the work I will be able to do as President of the Student Senate.
I’ve also founded and been the executive of many different clubs, this has given me the skills necessary to effectively delegate work, and manage time, which will be crucial as the President of the Student Senate. For example, starting the virtual chess club this year, I’ve organized an executive team which helps coordinate every chess meet, and allows our players to improve their game. These skills will allow me to create a Student Senate that is effective, I just need your vote, so Vote Umar!
Even during the pandemic and our quadmester system, I’ve helped to organize and run events virtually or with the limited physical access we have had, and try and normalize the school year. Despite the constrictions of this year, the events organized have been successful and engaging, and I believe I can bring that to the Student Senate.
